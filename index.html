    Armauraders: the Last Batallion Media Hub

    This app is intended as sort of a mobile press kit and media hub for the A:tLB franchise. The plan is to host a variety of related media, such as episode 1 of the motion comic, as well as all current pages of the print/web comic, images from the upcoming toy line, as well as a push enabled news section where the creators of the brand, can post the latest news about all things Armauraders.

    This project is intended for all iOS devices, but will probably be best suited to iPad, and newer iPhones.
